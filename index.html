ABOUT CSI

"Discover who we are and what sets us apart."

The Computer Society of India was established back in 1965 by IT professionals who wanted to organize their technical activities. They wanted to build a community that actively discusses the advancements in technology and believes in sharing knowledge and opportunities among others.

CSI works with a number of organizations overseas to promote technical activities. It plays an important role in helping to formulate IT strategy and planning for governments.

ABOUT CSI ITNU

"Hub of Techies"

Computer Society of India, Student Chapter, Nirma University was established in 2004. We have been awarded as the Best Student Chapter at National Level for the 2010 - 2011 batch.

We at CSI aim to constantly improve the technical culture of our university. We guide technology enthusiasts to develop their skills so that they can compete in the tech world and solve real life problems. Our events organized throughout the year see a lot of participation from in and out of the institute.

CSI has been very active year after year in organizing events and workshops. Technical events such as CUBIX and Hack-NU-Thon are our flagship events. Industry expert talks are organized by us to spark up the energy in students and make them aware of the real life applications of what they are learning. These sessions have also helped students
understand the current requirements of the industry.

We organize coding contests regularly in order to groom the problem solving skills of students. Also, daily challenges are given to students to help achieve consistency in their journey.

EVENTS

"Innovate. Code. Succeed."

CUBIX 

Every year, CSI organizes its flagship event CUBIX. CUBIX is a perfect amalgamation of fun events and technical activities. A variety of activities are conducted under this event including coding contests, development activities, treasure hunts etc.

Each activity aims to develop technical abilities of students along with a dash of fun and frolic. Team and individual events both constitute the list of events.

Some of the activities conducted as a part of Cubix 2022 include - Coder Hunt, Expect The Unexpected, SemiColon, AlgoTrivia and many more.

HACK-NU-THON

Hack-NU-Thon is a 36 hours long hackathon hosted to encounter new ideas and implementations for real life problems. Every year, CSI hosts this event in association with tech companies like DevFolio, Polygon and more.

An individual or a team can choose from the domains which are provided to them or they can come up with their own ideas for the problem statement.

After their registration and problem statement selection, each team is assigned a mentor who are industry experts so that, the participant can get more understanding of their ideas.

The final judgment is done based on many factors such as feasibility, scalability, implementation of the project etc. All these are reviewed by the industry experts.

Domains provided in Hackathon 2022 include - Health care, Ed-Tech, Business, BlockChain, Agriculture etc.

WORKSHOPS AND SPEAKER SESSIONS

CSI believes in widening its area and touching upon a variety of domains in the Computer Science field. The possible domains that can be explored are vast and hence, organizing a wide variety of workshops and speaker sessions is a great way to keep pace with this ever evolving field.

Numerous domains like Cyber Security, Quant Technology, Machine Learning, Web Development have been touched upon by eminent speakers from the industry. Guest speakers also include Nirma Alumni who have reached great heights in the industry.

Some notable speakers who delivered their talks include- Sandeep Jain (Founder and CEO of Geeks for Geeks), Aman Dhattarwal (famous YouTuber and founder of ApniKaksha and Apna College) etc.

INTERVIEW PREPARATION

Placements are an integral part of every student’s college life. CSI and its seniors have proven their legacy year after year in terms of their placement record. In addition to this, numerous factors affecting one’s placements are sought after by the club.

Competitive Programming is a great sport that enhances the logical reasoning and problem solving abilities of students. Computer Engineers are best known for their robust implementation skills and their analytical logic. Daily challenges and biweekly contests are the initiatives taken up by CSI to inculcate this culture in the university students.

CSI gives a lot of opportunities to its members wherein they can improve their soft skills like public speaking, event management, content writing, social media branding etc.

Interview preparation sessions are held before the onset of the placement season wherein last minute tips and tricks are discussed and seniors share their interview experiences company wise. These experiences help to realize the hiring process and requirements posed by every firm.
